Levo Privacy Policy

At Levo, we respect and protect your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Leave Management System. By using Levo, you agree to the collection and use of information as outlined in this policy.

Information We Collect

Levo collects personal information from employees and administrators, including:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Employee ID
  • Leave request details (e.g., dates, type of leave)
  • IP address and device information

Data Security

We take your privacy seriously. We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. These measures include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure login authentication
  • Regular security audits
  • Role-based access control

Sharing of Your Information

Levo does not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your data in the following circumstances:

  • If required by law or to comply with legal processes
  • If necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of Levo, its users, or the public
  • With third-party service providers that help us operate our system (e.g., payment processors, analytics providers)
