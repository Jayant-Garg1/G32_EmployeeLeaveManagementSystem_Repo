Levo Privacy Policy

At Levo, we respect and protect your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Leave Management System. By using Levo, you agree to the collection and use of information as outlined in this policy.

Information We Collect

Levo collects personal information from employees and administrators, including:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Employee ID
  • Leave request details (e.g., dates, type of leave)
  • IP address and device information

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To process leave requests and manage leave history
  • To improve the functionality of our leave management system
  • To communicate with you regarding your leave status and updates
  • To analyze usage patterns and enhance the platform experience
  • To provide customer support and respond to feedback

Data Security

We take your privacy seriously. We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. These measures include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure login authentication
  • Regular security audits
  • Role-based access control

Sharing of Your Information

Levo does not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your data in the following circumstances:

  • If required by law or to comply with legal processes
  • If necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of Levo, its users, or the public
  • With third-party service providers that help us operate our system (e.g., payment processors, analytics providers)

Cookies

Levo may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience. Cookies help us analyze user behavior and allow us to improve the website's functionality. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect your experience on the platform.

Your Data Protection Rights

You have the right to access, correct, update, or delete your personal information at any time. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@levo.com.

Additionally, you may request the following:

  • Access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Rectification of any inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion of your data, where applicable

Changes to this Privacy Policy

Levo reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we will notify users via email if significant changes are made. By continuing to use the platform after any changes to this policy, you accept the updated terms.
